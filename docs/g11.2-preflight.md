the at-Tawbah-era architecture proposal. The substrate

`rust_signature_canonicalization.py`, and
`rust_surface_extraction.py`.

prompt's T02 dispatch-table model.** The prompt's

language-keyed external dispatch with one-verifier-per-
language modules. The substrate at v0.11.7 instead
implements language whitelist + helper-module dispatch
inside a single `verification.py`.
